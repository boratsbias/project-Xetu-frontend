Our story

A small bridge. A big difference.

XETU — "সেতু" means bridge in Assamese — is exactly that: a bridge between the people of Golaghat and the drivers who keep the city moving. We started with one belief: getting a ride should be as easy as sending a message.

Today we connect residents with nearby eRickshaw drivers for a smooth, accessible ride experience, while putting more of every fare back into the local economy.

Vision

Beyond Golaghat. Across Assam. Then India.

We aim to expand XETU across Assam and India, bringing better urban mobility to more communities — one town, one district, one state at a time.

  • More towns across Upper Assam
  • Multi-language support (Assamese, Hindi, English)
  • Real-time ride tracking
  • Better tools and earnings for our drivers
